# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 1, 2026**

Stokpot ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Pantry Data:** Ingredients and quantities you add to track your pantry inventory
- **Meal Plans:** Recipes and meals you save to your meal plan
- **Shopping Lists:** Items you add to your shopping lists
- **Favorites:** Recipes you save to your favorites
- **Preferences:** Dietary preferences, serving sizes, and app settings

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Usage Data:** How you interact with the app (features used, screens viewed)
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system version, and app version
- **Crash Data:** Technical information to help us fix bugs and improve stability

### Information We Do NOT Collect
- We do not collect your name, email address, or contact information unless you contact us for support
- We do not collect your location data
- We do not collect payment information (all payments are processed securely by Apple)

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Provide and maintain the app's functionality
- Generate personalized meal plans and recipe suggestions
- Sync your data across your devices (if signed into iCloud)
- Improve the app and develop new features
- Fix bugs and ensure app stability

## Data Storage

### Local Storage
Most of your data (pantry, meal plans, shopping lists, favorites) is stored locally on your device.

### iCloud Sync
If you have iCloud enabled, your data may be synced across your Apple devices using Apple's CloudKit. This data is encrypted and stored according to Apple's privacy practices.

### Third-Party Services
We use the Spoonacular API to provide recipe search and discovery features. When you search for recipes, your search queries are sent to Spoonacular. Please review [Spoonacular's Privacy Policy](https://spoonacular.com/food-api/terms) for more information.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

We may share anonymized, aggregated data (such as total number of app users or popular recipe searches) that cannot be used to identify you.

## Data Retention

Your data remains on your device until you delete the app or clear app data. If you use iCloud sync, data is retained according to Apple's iCloud policies.

## Your Rights

You can:
- **Access** your data by viewing it within the app
- **Delete** your data by deleting items in the app or uninstalling the app
- **Disable iCloud Sync** in your device settings if you don't want data synced

## Children's Privacy

Stokpot is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us.

## Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
